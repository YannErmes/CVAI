## Desktop vs Mobile

### Desktop (Sidebar visible)
- Card appears on right side
- Always visible, stays put
- Full dialog visible when expanded

### Mobile (Sidebar collapsed)
- Card hidden by default
- Toggle sidebar button (bottom right)
- Open sidebar to see card
- Dialog takes full screen when tweaking
