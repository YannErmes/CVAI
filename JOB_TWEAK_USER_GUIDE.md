## What Each Tweak Does

### üéØ Summary Tweak
**Original**: Generic professional summary
**Tweaked**: Reworded to use keywords from the job description

**Example**:
```

After:  "Experienced React developer with expertise in AWS 
         microservices and team leadership"
```

### üéØ Skills Tweak
**Original**: Your full skills list
**Tweaked**: Reordered to show most relevant skills first for THIS job


(React moved to top because job emphasizes React)

### üéØ Experience Tweak

```
         using React and Node.js on AWS, handling 100K+ daily users"
```

## Common Questions

### Q: Does tweaking use my daily generation limit?
**A**: No! Only full CV generation counts against the 5/day limit. You can tweak unlimited times.

### Q: Can I tweak the same CV multiple times?
**A**: Yes! Click "Back" in the dialog to regenerate with different tweaks.

### Q: Will my original CV be changed?
**A**: Not until you click "Apply". You can always click "Discard" to close without changes.

### Q: What if I don't like the tweaks?
**A**: Click "Back" to regenerate, or click "Discard" to go back to your original CV.

### Q: Can I manually edit after tweaks are applied?
**A**: Absolutely! Go back to the edit form and manually refine any section.

### Q: Do tweaks work with all CV templates?
**A**: Yes! Tweaks update your content, which works with any template.

### Q: How long does tweaking take?
**A**: 2-3 seconds usually. Depends on job description length.

### Q: Can I see what tweaks I applied?
**A**: Yes! Just look at your form fields - the tweaked text is already there. You can always undo by regenerating from backup.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Tweak My CV" button is disabled/grayed out
**Solution**: Make sure you've typed something in the job description field

### Problem: Dialog shows error "API Key Required"
**Solution**: Go to Settings, add your Gemini API key, then try again

### Problem: AI tweaks don't seem relevant
**Solution**: 
- Try pasting the full job description instead of just the title
- Include key requirements (technologies, years of experience, etc.)
- Click "Back" and regenerate for alternative suggestions

### Problem: Tweaks keep reverting
**Solution**: Make sure you click "Apply" button, not "Back" or "Discard"

### Problem: I accidentally applied tweaks I didn't want
**Solution**:
- Go back to edit form
- Manually restore your original text
- Or: Close without saving and regenerate from last saved version
